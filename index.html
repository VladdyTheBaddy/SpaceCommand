Command Manual
Galactic survival guide for new commanders

🚀 GETTING STARTED

1. Build Extractors — Start with Metal Extractors and Quantum Processors. These generate your base resources automatically.

2. Research & Shipyard — Unlock technologies to build better ships. Ships let you explore, attack, and colonize.

3. Explore the Galaxy — Navigate to other systems to find NPCs, debris fields, and empty worlds to colonize.

4. Join a Faction — Choose Federation, Pirates, Corporate, or Alien Hive for unique bonuses and ships.

5. Build Defenses — Laser Batteries and Missile Silos protect your homeworld from raids.

⚔️ COMBAT

Attack — Standard fleet vs NPC combat. Good for farming resources and experience.

Siege — Multi-wave assault on fortified positions. Higher risk, higher reward. Requires strong fleets.

Bosses — Coordinate with alliance members to defeat galactic threats for massive loot.

Defense Platforms — Built in the Buildings tab under "Defense". They fire automatically during raids.

💰 ECONOMY

Resources — Metal (ore), Quantum (crystal), and Fuel (hydrogen) are produced by buildings. Build depots to increase storage caps.

Trade Routes — Set up automated convoys between your planets for passive income. Requires multiple colonies.

Marketplace — Sell excess ships and resources to other players for Credits.

Black Market — High-risk trades that reduce reputation but offer powerful illegal tech.

Taxes — Adjust tax rate in Empire. Higher taxes = more credits but lower morale. Rebellion risk below 20% morale.

🌍 EMPIRE MANAGEMENT

Planet Types — Terran (balanced), Ice (+fuel), Volcanic (+metal), Gas Giant (+fuel/crystal), Desert (+energy), Oceanic (+crystal).

Specialization — Designate planets as Industrial, Research, Military, or Mining hubs for bonus production.

Population — Grows naturally if morale is above 50%. Build housing (Terraforming Engine) to increase max population.

Officers — Hire governors with traits that boost specific stats. Refresh the pool periodically for better candidates.
