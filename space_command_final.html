📖 Quick Guide

Getting Started: Build mines to generate resources, construct ships in the Shipyard, and research technologies to advance your empire.

Combat: Send fleets to attack NPCs or other players. Check the Galaxy view for targets. Use Hermes Probes for espionage before attacking.

Missions: Complete daily missions for bonus rewards and resources.

Alliances: Join an alliance to coordinate with other players and share resources.

Credits: Premium currency for instant completions and exclusive items. Earn through combat or purchase in the store.
